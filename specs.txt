Spec sheet for Coin Combination program
1) Return 1 cent
	Input: 1 cent change
	Output: 1 penny
2) Return 5 cents
	Input: 5 cents change
	Output: 1 nickel
3) Return 10 cents
	Input: 10 cnets change
	Output 1 dime
4) Return 25 cents
	Input: 25 cents change
	Output: 1 quarter
5) Return 30 cents
	Input: 30 cents change
	Output: 1 quarter, 1 nickel
6) Return 40 cents
	Input: 40 cents change
	Output: 1 quarter, 1 dime, 1 nickel
7) Return 41 cents
	Input: 41 cents change
	Output: 1 quarter, 1 dime, 1 nickel, 1 penny
8) Return 99 cents
	Input: 99 cents change
	Output: 3 quarters, 2 dimes, 4 pennies
